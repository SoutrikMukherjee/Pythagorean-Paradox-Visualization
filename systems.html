Line Segment Common Divisor Visualization

Problem Setup

Given:

  • Line AB = 36 inches
  • Line CD = 10 inches
  • Line XY = unknown length

Constraint: Length of XY must satisfy mx = 36 and nx = 10, where m, n ∈ ℤ⁺

This means XY must be a common divisor of both 36 and 10.

Common divisors of 36 and 10: 1 and 2

Therefore, XY can be either 1 inch or 2 inches long.
